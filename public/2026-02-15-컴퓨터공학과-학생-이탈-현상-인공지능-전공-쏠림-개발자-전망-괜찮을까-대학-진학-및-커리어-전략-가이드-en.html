The Great Computer Science Exodus: Is AI the Future? Choosing the Right Major & Career Paths for Students

Students are showing less general interest in computer science while gaining interest in AI-specific majors and courses. Let's explore the trend and provide guidance on choosing the right path.

  • Decline in Popularity of Computer Science:
    • The popularity of computer science, which used to be a highly sought-after major, has relatively decreased recently.
    • This is analyzed as a result of a combination of factors such as intensified competition in the developer field, high workload, and the burden of rapidly changing technology trends.
  • The Rise of AI Majors:
    • Due to the development of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology and the expansion of AI adoption throughout society, interest in AI-related majors is exploding.
    • In particular, expectations for high-income occupations such as AI engineers and data scientists are greatly influencing students' choices.
  • Major Selection and Career Strategies:
    • The basic knowledge of computer science is still important. Even if you choose an AI major, it is essential to build a solid foundation in computer science.
    • It is necessary to secure competitiveness through participation in various projects, internship experiences, and continuous self-development.
    • It is important to design a career flexibly in line with changing market trends.

🔍 Deep Dive

While the AI field is expected to continue to grow, excessive concentration can intensify competition and lead to workforce imbalances. Greater synergy can be created when talents with the basics of computer science are integrated into the AI field. Therefore, universities should strengthen the convergence education courses of computer science and artificial intelligence, and students should carefully choose majors considering their aptitude and interests. This will contribute to the balanced development and innovation of the global IT industry.
