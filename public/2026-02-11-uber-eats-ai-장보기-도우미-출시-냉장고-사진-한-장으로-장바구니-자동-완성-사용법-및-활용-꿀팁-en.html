Published on: 2026-02-11

Uber Eats Cart Assistant: How to Use the AI Grocery Shopping Helper, Maximize Savings, and Automatically Fill Your Cart with Just a Fridge Photo!

  • Uber Eats has launched a groundbreaking AI feature called "Cart Assistant," which allows you to automatically fill your shopping cart using text prompts or a single image.

  • Key Features:

    • Text-Based Cart Generation: Simply enter a text description like "Things I need for a BBQ this weekend," and the AI will automatically add the necessary items to your cart.
    • Image-Based Cart Generation: Upload a photo of your refrigerator, and the AI will analyze the image, identify missing ingredients, and add them to your cart.
    • Personalized Recommendations: Receive product recommendations optimized for you based on your past order history and preferences.
    • Easy Modification: Freely delete items added by the AI or change the quantities.

  • How to Use:

    1. Update your Uber Eats app to the latest version.
    2. Go to the grocery store section.
    3. Select the "Cart Assistant" feature.
    4. Enter a text prompt or upload a photo.
    5. Review the cart generated by the AI and make any necessary modifications.
    6. Complete your order.

  • Pro Tips:

    • The more specific your text prompt, the more accurate the results provided by the AI. (Example: "1 head of organic romaine lettuce, 12 antibiotic-free eggs, 1 liter of low-fat milk")
    • Take fridge photos in bright lighting and from various angles to improve the AI's recognition rate.
    • If you don't like the products recommended by the AI, click the "See other options" button to view a wider range of products.
