  • Key Takeaways:
    • Fractal Analytics specializes in data analytics and AI solutions.

    • A general sell-off in Indian software stocks is believed to have dampened investor sentiment.

  • While investors remain interested in the growth potential of the Indian AI market, they are expressing concerns about short-term volatility.
