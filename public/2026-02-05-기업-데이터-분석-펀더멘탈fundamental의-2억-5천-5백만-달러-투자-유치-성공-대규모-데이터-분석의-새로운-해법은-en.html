    • Mid-Sized Enterprises: Supports the establishment and training for data analysis platforms, facilitating business insight extraction.

    • AI-Powered Data Analysis Engine Development: To provide faster and more accurate data analysis results.
    • Global Market Expansion: Strengthening localization strategies for overseas expansion.
    • Talent Acquisition: Expanding the recruitment of data analysis experts and developers.
  • Successful Implementation Strategies for Fundamental's Data Analysis Solutions:
    • Clear Goal Setting: Defining the specific problems to be solved through data analysis.
    • Data Quality Assurance: Ensuring accurate and reliable data collection.
    • Expert Collaboration: Seeking assistance from data analysis professionals to interpret and utilize analysis results.
    • Continuous Improvement: Making ongoing improvements and optimizations based on data analysis findings.
