  • Duna, founded by ex-Stripe engineers, has secured a ₩39 billion (approximately $30 million USD) investment, signaling a major disruption in the business identity verification solution market.
  • Duna offers solutions that enable companies to quickly and accurately verify business identities, with a strong presence in the European market.
  • Choosing the Right Business Identity Verification Solution: A Guide
    • Accuracy: Verify the database's up-to-dateness and the verification processes. Inaccurate information can lead to business risks.
    • Speed: Rapid identity verification is essential for improving customer experience. Ensure the solution offers real-time verification capabilities.
    • Scalability: The solution must be scalable with the company's growth. Consider whether it supports identity verification in various countries and regions.
    • API Integration: Seamless integration with existing systems improves operational efficiency. Check if the solution supports easy API integration.
    • Cost-Effectiveness: Compare and analyze whether the solution offers reasonable pricing relative to its features and performance.

    • Investor Collaboration: Securing growth momentum through investment attraction and expanding the business through close collaboration with investors.
    • Strong Teamwork: Forming a team with experienced and technically skilled individuals to increase the chances of business success.
