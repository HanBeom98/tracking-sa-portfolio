Siri Update Delayed: Not Coming in iOS 26.4? 🤔 Latest News and User Guidance

  • Apple's Siri update has reportedly been delayed again.
  • The new Siri, initially expected to be released with the iOS 26.4 update in March, will now be rolled out gradually.
  • Some features might be postponed until the May iOS update or even the iOS 27 release in September.
  • There's no official explanation for the delay, but it's presumed to ensure feature completeness and stability.
  • Users who have been eagerly awaiting the Siri update might be disappointed, but this decision seems necessary for delivering a stable service.
  • Despite the delay, Apple is expected to continue its efforts to improve Siri's performance and add new features.
  • Users should regularly check for iOS updates and monitor Apple's official announcements.
  • During the update delay, it's recommended to leverage existing Siri features to minimize inconvenience.
  • If you encounter any Siri-related issues, you can seek assistance through the Apple Support Community or an Apple Store.
