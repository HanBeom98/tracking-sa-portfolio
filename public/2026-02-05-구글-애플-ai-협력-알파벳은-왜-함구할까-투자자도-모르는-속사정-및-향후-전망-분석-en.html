Google and Apple AI Collaboration: Why Alphabet's Silence? An In-Depth Analysis of Hidden Motives and Investment Strategies

  • The surprise announcement of AI collaboration between Google and Apple has left investors and the market confused, especially given Alphabet's (Google's parent company) conspicuous silence.

  • This collaboration centers around integrating Google's Gemini AI model into Apple devices, promising users a more powerful AI experience on iPhones and other Apple products.

  • Reasons Behind Alphabet's Silence:

    • Fear of Intensified Competition: Alphabet might believe that this collaboration could weaken Google's AI market dominance in the long run. Strategic silence could indicate a focus on developing its own AI models independently.
    • Potential Internal Conflicts: Disagreements within Google regarding the collaboration with Apple are possible. A consensus on collaboration terms or future strategy may not have been reached yet.
    • Negotiation Tactic: This could be a deliberate strategy to gain a stronger negotiating position with Apple, refraining from official comments to enhance bargaining power.

  • What Investors Need to Know:

    • Alphabet's silence doesn't necessarily mean opposition to the collaboration. It may reflect a strategic move to strengthen its own AI technology competitiveness in the long term.
    • Avoid overreacting to short-term stock fluctuations. Instead, closely monitor Alphabet's AI technology development roadmap and market strategy.
    • Pay attention to Microsoft's actions. Their collaboration with OpenAI has established a strong foothold in the AI market, making them a formidable competitor to Alphabet.

  • Future Outlook:

    • Alphabet is likely to concentrate on improving the performance of its own AI model, Gemini. They will leverage AI technology in various sectors like cloud, search, and autonomous driving to secure market competitiveness.
    • The collaboration with Apple will likely continue, but the scope and terms of the partnership could change. Alphabet will strive to negotiate in a way that maximizes its own benefits.
    • The AI market is poised for even fiercer competition. It's crucial to observe the technological rivalry and strategic shifts of major IT companies like Alphabet, Apple, and Microsoft.
