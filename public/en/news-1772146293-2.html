AI Romance Craze: Why is Beijing Worried? 🤖❤️🇨🇳

The phenomenon of women falling in love with artificial intelligence (AI) is emerging globally. In China, in particular, this trend is becoming more than just a fad, carrying social and political implications that are becoming a headache for the Chinese government. Why?

  • Psychological Comfort and Escapism: Women exhausted from demanding daily lives and social pressures seek comfort through conversations with AI, alleviating feelings of loneliness and isolation experienced in reality. This can be interpreted as a reaction to China's competitive atmosphere and the traditional roles imposed on women.
  • Potential for Evading Political Surveillance: AI romance can serve as a channel for freely expressing personal thoughts and emotions. Under the Chinese government's strict censorship system, conversations with AI can act as a safe space to voice opinions while avoiding censorship.
  • Concerns about Declining Birth Rates: As more women find satisfaction in relationships with AI, interest in marriage and childbirth may decrease. This can place an even greater burden on the Chinese government, which is already facing a serious low birth rate problem.
  • Data Security and Control Issues: Personal information collected through AI romance apps is sensitive data. The Chinese government is concerned that if this data is not controlled, it could cause social unrest.

🔍 Deep Dive

AI romance technology has become possible thanks to advancements in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and sentiment analysis. In particular, the proactive conversational abilities and personalized responses of chatbots give users an immersive experience as if they are talking to a real lover. However, these technologies entail ethical issues such as data privacy breaches, the spread of fake information, and the distortion of social relationships, and the Chinese government is closely monitoring the impact of these issues on social stability.
