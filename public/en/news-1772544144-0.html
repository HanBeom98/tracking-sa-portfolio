OpenAI's Pentagon Deal: The Beginning of the End for AI?

OpenAI, they've completely lost it. Where did their mantra of "AI for the benefit of humanity" go? Now they're joining hands with the Pentagon to develop killer weapons? This isn't just a collaboration; it's a terrifying signal of the ethical collapse of AI technology. Blinded by money, they've chosen the path to their own destruction.

According to the New York Times report, OpenAI has amended its AI deal with the Pentagon. While they superficially frame it as cooperation for "safety" and "security," the true intention is obvious. They aim to leverage the Pentagon's massive funding to jump into the LLM development race for military use.

This isn't just about participating in a few projects. OpenAI emerged proclaiming the "democratization" of AI technology. They presented the ideal of creating an ecosystem where anyone can freely utilize and develop AI technology. But collaborating with the Pentagon completely betrays that ideal. By participating in closed government projects, they are abandoning the openness and transparency of technology themselves.

A more serious problem is that this collaboration amplifies the potential for AI technology to be abused. The Pentagon is already utilizing AI in various military operations, such as drone strikes and surveillance systems. If OpenAI's technology is integrated into these systems, the consequences are unimaginable. AI, beyond human control, could malfunction or be exposed to hacking attacks, leading to the killing of innocent civilians.

Some argue that "safety measures" have been put in place, but who would believe that? The Pentagon will do anything to win a war. Ethical considerations are of no concern to them. Ultimately, OpenAI's technology will be used to develop killer weapons, and humanity may face the tragedy of destroying itself with AI.

There have been similar cases in the past. During World War II, scientists grappled with tremendous ethical dilemmas while participating in the development of the atomic bomb. What was the result? Hundreds of thousands of innocent civilians lost their lives to nuclear bombs. Has OpenAI learned nothing from history?

Moreover, OpenAI's competitors are already actively collaborating with the Pentagon. Palantir is supporting military operations through its data analysis platform, and Anduril is developing autonomous drone systems for border surveillance. Some analyze that OpenAI has partnered with the Pentagon to avoid falling behind in the competition, but this is just a cowardly excuse. They have sold the future of humanity for a few more bucks.

OpenAI, you are no longer the hope of AI technology. You are just a money-hungry corporation. Your choice will darken the future of AI technology and endanger humanity. It's not too late. Immediately stop collaborating with the Pentagon and strive for the ethical development of AI technology. Otherwise, you will be recorded as criminals in history.

In my view, OpenAI has already crossed the point of no return. We must monitor their future actions. And if OpenAI's technology is used in killer weapons, we must resist strongly. To protect the future of humanity.
