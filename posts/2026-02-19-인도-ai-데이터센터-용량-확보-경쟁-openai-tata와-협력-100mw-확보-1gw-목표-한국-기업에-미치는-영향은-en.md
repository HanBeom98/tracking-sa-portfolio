# OpenAI's India Data Center Strategy: How Tata Collaboration Secures 100MW (and 1GW Plans) & What It Means for Global AI Infrastructure

- The company plans to scale this capacity up to 1GW, indicating significant investment in AI capabilities within the Indian market.
- OpenAI intends to open new offices in Mumbai and Bengaluru later this year, further solidifying its presence in India.
- This expansion highlights the growing importance of India as a key market for AI development and deployment.
