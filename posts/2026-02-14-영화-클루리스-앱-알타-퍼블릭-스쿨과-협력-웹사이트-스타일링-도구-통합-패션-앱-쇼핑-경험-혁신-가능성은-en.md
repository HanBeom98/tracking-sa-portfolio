# Clueless App Alta Partners with Public School: Revolutionizing Fashion App Shopping with Website Styling Tools?

- Styling app Alta, inspired by the movie 'Clueless,' is collaborating with New York fashion brand Public School to integrate styling tools into their website.
- Alta is an app that helps users easily find and coordinate outfits that match their style and preferences.
- Through this collaboration, Public School website visitors will be able to enjoy a more personalized shopping experience by utilizing Alta's styling tools.
- Starting with its partnership with Public School, Alta plans to expand its partnerships with various brands to increase the integration of styling tools within websites.
- This aims to go beyond simple clothing shopping and provide the styling experience that was previously only available on the app, thereby increasing customer satisfaction in online shopping malls.
- Alta plans to enhance its AI-based style recommendation features in the future to provide even more sophisticated and convenient styling services.
