# Hollywood Warns of Copyright Infringement with Seedance 2.0 AI Video Generator: A Cautionary Tale for Aspiring Filmmakers

- Hollywood is strongly opposing the new AI video model 'Seedance 2.0', labeling it as a tool being rapidly exploited for "blatant" copyright infringement.
- Hollywood organizations argue that Seedance 2.0 severely infringes upon creators' rights by enabling the easy creation of new content based on existing movies and video content.
- Of particular concern is Seedance 2.0's ability to allow users to specify particular movie scenes or characters to create similar videos.
- Hollywood is threatening strong responses, including legal action, and emphasizing the need to strengthen regulations regarding AI technology development and usage.
- Aspiring filmmakers, whether university students or job seekers, must always be mindful of copyright issues when using AI technology and pursue creative activities legally.
