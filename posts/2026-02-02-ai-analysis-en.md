# How to Join Ring's "Search Party" to Find Lost Dogs Even Without a Ring Camera: Strengthening Community Safety Nets

- Now, you can participate in the 'Search Party' to find missing dogs and contribute to building a local community safety net, even without a Ring camera.
- Ring, Amazon's smart home security brand, previously allowed only Ring camera users to participate in the 'Search Party.'
- This update allows users without Ring cameras to share information about missing dogs and cooperate in the search through the Ring app.
- Ring app users can share information about missing dogs with neighbors and report sightings immediately.
- Ring is expected to become an effective community collaboration tool for finding missing dogs and ensuring their safety.
- Expanding participation in the 'Search Party' will raise awareness of safety in the local community and contribute more actively to solving the problem of missing dogs.
