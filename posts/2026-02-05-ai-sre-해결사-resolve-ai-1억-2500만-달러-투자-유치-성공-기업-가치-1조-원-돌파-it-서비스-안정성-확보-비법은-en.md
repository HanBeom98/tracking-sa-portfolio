# Resolve AI Secures $125 Million Investment: Achieving Unicorn Status with AI-Powered SRE & Ensuring IT Service Reliability

- Resolve AI, an AI-based Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) solution provider, has successfully raised $125 million in funding, achieving a valuation of over 1 trillion KRW (approximately $750 million USD), marking its entry into unicorn status.
- Resolve AI provides an innovative solution that leverages AI to enhance the stability of IT systems and quickly resolve failures.
- Key features include:
    - **Anomaly Detection:** Real-time detection of abnormal behavior in IT systems based on AI.
    - **Automated Problem Resolution:** AI automatically analyzes the root cause of problems and suggests solutions.
    - **Preventive Maintenance:** Prediction of potential issues and proactive responses through historical data analysis.
- Resolve AI's solution minimizes losses due to IT service disruptions and maximizes IT operational efficiency.
- The increasing complexity of cloud environments has heightened the demand for IT system stability, driving further interest in Resolve AI's solutions.
- This investment will enable Resolve AI to accelerate the development and expansion of its AI-based SRE solutions.
