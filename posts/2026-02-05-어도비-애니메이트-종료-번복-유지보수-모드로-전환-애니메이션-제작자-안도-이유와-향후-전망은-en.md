# Adobe Animate CC Reversal! Maintenance Mode Announced: Why Animators Are Relieved & Alternative Software Compared

Adobe has reversed its decision to discontinue Animate CC, a popular animation tool, and will instead transition it to maintenance mode. This announcement has been met with relief from animators who were initially concerned about the abrupt termination. Here's a breakdown of the decision, its implications, and alternative software options:

- **Why the Reversal? Adobe Animate CC Stays Alive:**
    - Adobe reconsidered the market landscape and user feedback, leading to the reversal.
    - Strong opposition from users played a crucial role in the decision to maintain Animate CC.
    - Maintenance mode provides a stable environment for existing users and reduces concerns about project transitions.

- **Maintenance Mode: What Does It Mean?**
    - While new feature development will be limited, maintenance mode ensures:
        - Ongoing stability improvements.
        - Bug fixes and security patches.
    - Adobe will provide detailed information about future updates and support policies.

- **Animator Relief: Why Were They Concerned?**
    - Animate CC has been a long-standing industry-standard tool.
    - Many animators possess advanced skills and expertise in Animate CC.
    - The termination would have forced workflow changes, new software learning, and potential compatibility issues.
    - Maintenance mode minimizes disruption and allows animators to continue working smoothly.

- **Animate CC Alternatives: A Comparative Analysis**
    - Several alternative animation software packages are available:
        - **Toon Boom Harmony:** A professional animation tool with robust features and diverse workflows.
        - **Clip Studio Paint:** Renowned for its drawing capabilities, with continuously improving animation features.
        - **OpenToonz:** A free, open-source software known for its use by Studio Ghibli.
    - It is crucial to compare the pros, cons, pricing, and features of each software to find the best fit.
