# Can Sarvam AI Models Run on Feature Phones? Exploring India's Lightweight AI Revolution for Edge Devices

- India's Sarvam is aiming to bring its AI models to feature phones, cars, and smart glasses by utilizing edge models that are extremely compact.
- These edge models take up only megabytes of space, allowing them to run on most phones with existing processors and function offline.
- Key benefits:
    - **Compact Size:** Models are designed to be incredibly small, enabling operation on low-powered devices.
    - **Offline Functionality:** Enables AI features to be used without an internet connection.
    - **Versatile Applications:** Can be integrated into various devices, including feature phones, cars, and smart glasses.
- This approach potentially democratizes access to AI technology for users worldwide, especially in areas with limited connectivity.

## 🔍 Deep Dive
- Sarvam's lightweight AI models represent a significant advancement in edge computing, offering the potential to revolutionize various industries. By enabling AI functionality on resource-constrained devices, the company opens doors for new applications in areas like healthcare, education, and transportation, especially in developing countries. The focus on offline capabilities also addresses concerns around data privacy and security.
