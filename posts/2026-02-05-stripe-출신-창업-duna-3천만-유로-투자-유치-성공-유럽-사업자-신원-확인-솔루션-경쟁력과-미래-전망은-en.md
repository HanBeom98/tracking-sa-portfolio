#Stripe Alumni Startup Duna Secures €30 Million Funding: How to Achieve GDPR Compliance and Competitive Advantage in European Business Verification

- Duna, a fintech company founded by ex-Stripe employees, has successfully raised €30 million in funding. Duna specializes in providing Know Your Business (KYB) solutions for European businesses.
- Duna's key competitive advantage lies in offering efficient identity verification services while adhering to the stringent General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in Europe.
- While traditional KYB solutions are often complex and time-consuming, Duna enables fast and accurate identity verification through automated processes and data analysis.
- This investment will enable Duna to further strengthen its presence in the European market and accelerate the development of new features and service expansion.
- Duna's solution can be utilized in various fields, including online financial transactions, e-commerce, and SaaS, enhancing the efficiency of business identity verification and contributing to cost reduction and risk management for businesses.
- Notably, Duna integrates and analyzes information from diverse sources, such as business registration data, credit information, and compliance data, to assess business credibility and provide robust capabilities for detecting fraudulent activities.
- Adopting Duna's solution simplifies the complex procedures for GDPR compliance, reduces time and costs through automated workflows, and enhances anti-money laundering (AML) capabilities.
