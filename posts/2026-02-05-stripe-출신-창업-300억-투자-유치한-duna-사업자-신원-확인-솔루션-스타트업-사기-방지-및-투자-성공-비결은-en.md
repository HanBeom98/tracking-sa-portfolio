# How Duna, a Startup Founded by Ex-Stripe Engineers, Secured a ₩30 Billion Investment: Understanding Their Business Identity Verification Solution and Fraud Prevention Strategies for Startups

- Duna, a startup founded by former Stripe engineers specializing in business identity verification solutions, has successfully secured a ₩30 billion investment.
- Duna's solution helps startups and enterprises prevent fraud and comply with regulations by verifying business registration information, credit information, and sanction lists in real-time.
- Key features include:
    - **Automated Identity Verification:** Automates the verification of business information, saving time and costs compared to manual verification processes.
    - **Real-time Risk Assessment:** Provides immediate fraud risk detection and alerts through real-time data analysis.
    - **Customized Workflows:** Allows businesses to customize workflows to create efficient identity verification processes tailored to their specific needs.
    - **Regulatory Compliance Support:** Supports compliance with regulations such as Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements.
- Strategies for preventing startup fraud:
    - **Thorough Identity Verification from the Start:** Verify the identities of transaction partners, investors, and customers thoroughly from the outset to prevent potential risks.
    - **Data-Driven Decision Making:** Make decisions based on data analysis to identify and manage risks effectively.
    - **Strengthen Security Systems:** Establish security systems to protect personal information and prevent data breaches, and conduct regular audits.
    - **Continuous Education and Training:** Provide employees with education and training on fraud prevention and regulatory compliance.
- Reasons for investment success:
    - **Clear Problem Solving:** Focused on solving the well-defined problem of business identity verification.
    - **Innovative Technology:** Secured differentiated technological capabilities, including automated identity verification and real-time risk assessment.
    - **Proven Team Capabilities:** Assembled a skilled team led by founders with a background at Stripe.
    - **Growth Potential:** High potential for business expansion alongside the growth of the startup ecosystem.
