# Duna Secures $30 Million Funding: How This Stripe-Alumni Startup is Revolutionizing European Business Verification & What it Means for Global Expansion

- Duna, founded by former Stripe engineers, has successfully raised ₩30 billion (approximately $30 million USD) in funding, disrupting the European business identity verification solution market.
- Duna provides a solution that helps companies across Europe easily and quickly verify business registration information. This is an essential service for businesses considering expansion into the complex European market.

- **Key Success Factors:**
    - **Stripe-Experienced Founding Team:** Leveraging their Fintech industry experience to accurately identify market needs and enhance technical completeness.
    - **European Market Specialization:** Simplifying the complex European business registration system, lowering the barrier to entry for companies.
    - **Strong Technological Prowess:** Ensuring credibility through real-time data updates and accurate information provision.

- **Investment Strategy Analysis:**
    - Duna plans to use the secured investment to focus on expanding its market share in Europe and advancing its solution.
    - It is expected to invest in enhancing data analysis and identity verification accuracy using AI technology.
    - Furthermore, the company aims to secure a competitive advantage by strengthening customer support and developing new features.

- **Solution Use Cases:**
    - Online Shopping Malls: Preventing fraud and enhancing credibility through seller identity verification.
    - Financial Institutions: Simplifying and increasing the efficiency of Know Your Business (KYB) processes for corporate clients.
    - Government Agencies: Crackdown on illegal businesses and secure tax revenue.
